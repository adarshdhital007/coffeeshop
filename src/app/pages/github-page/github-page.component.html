Features

Uses Multiple Routing

This project takes advantage of Angular's powerful routing capabilities to provide a seamless and user-friendly navigation experience. Whether you're exploring the menu, managing your cart, or checking out, our multi-routing system ensures that you can effortlessly move between different sections of the Coffee Shop app.

Cart Add/Removal

Easily enhance your coffee experience by adding your favorite brews to your cart with a simple click. Say goodbye to complex ordering processes. Our intuitive interface empowers you to customize your coffee selections effortlessly, ensuring your perfect cup is just a click away. Plus, enjoy the convenience of quick service.

Bootstrap Implementation

I've incorporated Bootstrap to elevate your browsing experience. My commitment to responsive design means you can enjoy the Coffee Shop application seamlessly across a variety of devices and screen sizes. Enjoy the perfect blend of style and functionality as my implementation ensures responsiveness.
